CALVARY ASSEMBLIES OF GOD HISTORY

CALVARY ASSEMBLIES OF GOD CHURCH GHANA was established in the year 1995 and in the month of February. Which was founded by UPPER ROOM ASSEMBLIES OF GOD in Kwesimintsim-Takoradi by the Senior Pastor Reverend Adu Gyamfi.
The church was a vibrant church and they started small in a classroom of a school, by and by later hired a place in Assakae-Takoradi and of which became their firm grounds, their current place of worship. The church grew and was therefore handed over to Rev. Patrick Gyaakyi Mills who then transefered from Apowa Assemblies of God church to head the church. He served the church very well and later retired in the year 2014. Thereafter, a new pastor took over by the decision of the Executive Presbytery of the Region (Western) and also the committee of the District, all decided to bring Rev. Benjamin Eshun to continue to batton of ministry of The retired Rev. Patrick Gyaakyi Mills in the year 2015. The church is still growing largely and bountiful both physically and spiritually.
